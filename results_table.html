Why does 100% regime confidence cause low fusion confidence for HDFC? The HMM is 100% certain the market is in a BEAR regime. However, regression models are predicting an UP move (+1.07%). This direct contradiction between regime signal and price prediction triggers the CONFLICT flag, applying a 25% penalty to fusion confidence. The system is correctly expressing uncertainty — it cannot reconcile a BEAR regime with an UP prediction. This is a feature, not a bug.
